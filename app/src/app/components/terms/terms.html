2 Game Rules & Scoring System

Prediction Submission

  • Participants can predict the score for each match before its scheduled kick-off time (in IST).
  • Once a match has kicked off, predictions for that match are permanently locked and cannot be modified.
  • Each prediction must include: the predicted home score, the predicted away score, and a predicted first goal scorer.
  • For knockout stage matches that result in a draw, participants must additionally predict the penalty shootout winner and the match resolution time.

Points Allocation

Criteria Points
Exact Score — Predicted score matches the final result exactly 30
Correct Result — Predicted the correct outcome (win/draw) but not the exact score 10
Correct Goal Scorer — Predicted first goal scorer appears in the actual scorers list 10
Correct Resolution Time (Knockout only) — Predicted correct match resolution time (Normal Time / Extra Time / Penalty Shootout) 10
Incorrect Prediction — Wrong match outcome 0

Leaderboard & Rankings

  • A live leaderboard ranks all participants by total accumulated points.
  • Points are recalculated in real-time whenever an administrator enters a completed match result.
  • In case of a points tie, participants are ranked alphabetically by display name.
  • Rank change indicators (▲ / ▼) show position movement after each match completion.
